# HVAC CRM/PBX - Customer Retention & Maintenance Protection Strategy

## Core Philosophy

> We installed it. We know it best. Let us help you protect your investment.

The installation should be the **beginning of a 20-year customer relationship**, not the end of a sale.

Most HVAC companies today operate reactively:

```text
Install → disappear → compete for the customer again years later
```

The goal is to transform them into lifecycle businesses:

```text
Install
 ↓
Retain
 ↓
Maintain
 ↓
Service
 ↓
Repair
 ↓
Replace
 ↓
Repeat
```

# Maintenance Protection Plan

Instead of selling an "extended labour warranty", offer a **Maintenance Protection Plan**.

**Recommended:** Have legal counsel review any warranty terminology, especially in Canada.

## Pricing

- $150 per service visit
- 2 visits per year
  - Spring: AC / Heat Pump maintenance
  - Fall: Furnace maintenance

**Total: $300/year**

## Included Benefits

- Seasonal preventative maintenance
- Priority scheduling
- Discounted repairs
- Labour coverage while actively participating
- Digital service history
- Filter replacement reminders

---

# Labour Protection Model

Each completed maintenance visit extends labour protection for another 6 months.

Example:

```text
April maintenance
    ↓
Labour protection active until October

October maintenance
    ↓
Labour protection active until April
```

If a customer skips a maintenance visit:

```text
Protection expires
```

This creates a natural incentive to stay engaged.

# CRM Automation Opportunities

The system should proactively manage customer retention.

### 60 days before expiration

- Create a follow-up task

### 30 days before expiration

- Send email/SMS reminders

### 14 days before expiration

- Create a CSR call task

### Expired

- Move customer into an "At Risk" retention queue

The AI system could also generate call scripts for staff.

# Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)

This shifts HVAC companies from project-based revenue to predictable recurring revenue.

Example:

```text
1,000 customers

300 enrolled (30%)

$300/year

= $90,000/year recurring revenue
```

This does not include:

- Repair work
- Accessories
- Indoor air quality products
- Future replacements
